Opening Script (19:02)

Good evening President, fellow toastmasters and respected guests. My name is Kirin Mok. I am pleased to be the timer of tonight's meeting. To conduct the meeting smoothly and on schedule, I will monitor the time and remind you during prepared speeches, table topics and individual evaluations.

Let me explain the three timing signals.

Green Card: When you see the Green Card, it means you have reached the minimum time requirement.

Yellow Card: When you see the Yellow Card:
- For Manual speech speakers, you have 1 minute left.
- For Table Topic speakers and evaluators, you have 30 seconds left.

Red Card: When you see the Red Card, it means your time is up. You must finish within 30 seconds.

If you speak overtime, you will be disqualified and are not eligible for awards.

I will report the time of each participant at the end of the meeting and let you know if your presentation was within the allotted time frame.

Back to you, Toastmaster.
